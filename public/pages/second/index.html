On Ozon Bank’s page in the Ozon Mobile App, there is a promo information about key benefits of holding an Ozon Card. The top section includes an image of Ozon Bank card and financial icons. There are highlited benefits of the card below:

Free service and cashback system – clients save up to 25% outside Ozon and up to 30% on marketplace; Installment with Ozon Bank for clients purchases; Saving account - 22% per annum for the first 2 months if the client opening a saving account for the first time. Then 15% annually. To issue the card, the client can press button at the bottom of the window.

To exit this page, the user need to press the arrow button in the top left corner.
